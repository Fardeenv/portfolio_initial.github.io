Domain Description

Starlink is a satellite internet constellation operated by SpaceX, aimed at providing high-speed, low-latency internet access globally, especially in remote and underserved regions. Leveraging a mesh of thousands of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites, Starlink revolutionizes modern communication infrastructure by reducing dependence on traditional ground-based networks. It supports industries like maritime, aviation, military, and rural broadband through scalable, resilient, and efficient satellite-based connectivity solutions.
